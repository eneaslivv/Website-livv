· Founder questions

Everything you want to ask before the call.

Do I own the code and the IP?

100%. GitHub org is yours. All IP transfers on final payment. No revenue share, no equity strings, no hidden licenses. Your cap table stays clean.

Will my future CTO hate the codebase?

No — we write the code we'd want to inherit. TypeScript everywhere, proper migrations, tests on critical paths, clean folder structure, documented. 6 of our MVPs handed off to full-time teams with zero rewrite.

What about after the MVP launches?

30-day bug-fix warranty is included. After that, most founders slide into our fractional retainer ($4.9k/mo) until they raise and hire. Or we just do a clean handoff — your call.

Do you take equity instead of cash?

We don't recommend it — equity is your most expensive currency. We do offer deferred payment structures for founders raising within 60 days of launch, on a case-by-case basis.

What if I don't know exactly what to build yet?

That's what the product sprint is for. 2 days, $1,900, credited against your MVP if you move forward. You leave with a scoped v1, clickable prototype, and a shipping plan.
