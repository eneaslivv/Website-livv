Ecommerce founder questions

The honest answers.

Do you work with my existing Shopify store or only new builds?+
Both. About 60% of our engagements are redesigns or CRO overhauls of existing stores. We'll audit what you have, keep what's working, and rebuild what's dragging conversion down.
Can you migrate me from WooCommerce / Wix / Squarespace?+
Yes. We do full migrations to Shopify or headless stacks. Product catalogs, customer data, order history, SEO redirects — all handled.
Do you handle paid ad creatives too?+
Yes. We produce static and motion creatives specifically for Meta, TikTok and YouTube. Most often bundled into a monthly retainer.
What if I don't have product photography yet?+
We can art-direct shoots remotely or produce 3D product renders and AI-assisted imagery in-house. Many of our launch films use zero live shots.
How do you measure success?+
We agree on KPIs before we start — usually conversion rate, AOV, bounce rate and page speed. You'll get a post-launch report at day 30 and day 90.
