· Brand questions

Everything to ask before the audit call.

What's the free store audit?

A 30-min call + a 5-min personalized Loom. We audit your PDP, cart, checkout, ad → landing funnel, and Core Web Vitals. You get a ranked punch-list of revenue leaks — whether or not you work with us.

Do you do headless / Hydrogen?

Yes. About 40% of our new builds are Hydrogen. We'll tell you honestly whether your volume + team make it worth it — most sub-$10M brands don't need it, and we'll say so.

Can you work alongside our media buyer / agency?

That's most of our work. Your media buyer sends creative and angles; we turn them into shipping-speed landing pages. We plug into their Slack, their Notion, their reporting.

How do you measure success?

Revenue. Conversion rate. LCP / INP. We set baselines before we start, track them in GA4 + Triple Whale, and report monthly. If a change didn't help, we revert it — no attachment.

What if I'm not on Shopify?

We also work with BigCommerce, WooCommerce, custom Next.js commerce, and Webflow Ecommerce. If you're on Wix or Squarespace — we'll usually recommend a migration path.
