Agency partnership FAQ

The honest answers.

Will my clients ever know you exist?+
Only if you want them to. We operate under NDA + MSA. Our designers and devs use your branded email, show up to your standups under your name, and deliver files formatted in your templates.
Can you work directly with our clients if we want?+
Yes. Some partners embed us as "their team." Others prefer us fully invisible. Both models work — we adapt to what protects your client relationship.
What do margins look like when partnering with LIVV?+
Partner agencies typically bill their clients 2.5×–3.5× what we bill them, maintaining 55–70% gross margin. We'll walk you through case-specific math on our intro call.
Do you compete for our clients?+
Never. We sign non-solicitation clauses in our MSA. We don't market to your client list, don't accept inbound from them, and don't credit NDA work publicly.
What stacks and tools do you support?+
Design: Figma, Adobe suite, Rive, Cinema 4D, After Effects. Dev: Webflow, Framer, Shopify, Next.js, React Native, Flutter, Supabase, Vercel. AI: Claude, OpenAI, custom agents. We also pick up client-specific stacks on request.
How quickly can we start?+
NDA same day, MSA within 3 business days, first scoped project kickoff within 7 days. We've had partners go from cold intro to first shipped project in 10 days.
