· The fine print

Everything you want to ask before the call.

How exactly does the white-label work?

Strict NDA from day one. We use your email domain, your Figma workspace, your GitHub. We never contact your client. Delivery is through your channels, with your branding.

Do you work on retainer or per-project?

Both. Most agencies start per-project to test us, then move to a monthly retainer once they see the output. No lock-in contracts — ever.

What if I need something outside your usual stack?

We cover 90% of modern stacks (React, Next, Webflow, Framer, Shopify, Supabase, Flutter, AI/LLM). If it's outside — we'll tell you upfront, and often we can still find the right senior freelancer under our management.

How fast can you start?

Book a call today — first deliverable can land on your desk within 48 hours. For full projects, we typically kick off within 3–5 business days once the brief is signed.

What does this actually cost?

Landing pages from $1,900. Full websites from $6,000. SaaS/app builds from $14,000. Retainers start at $2,900/mo for ~40 hours of senior design + dev.
