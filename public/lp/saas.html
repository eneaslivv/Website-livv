SaaS founder questions

The honest answers.

Do you replace our engineering team, or augment it?+
Almost always augment. Our typical engagement is 2–3 LIVV seniors working alongside your existing team, owning a surface (onboarding, billing, admin) or a feature stream.
How is your pricing so much lower than hiring FTEs?+
LATAM cost base. Our team trained in and ship for US/EU SaaS. You're not paying for SF/NYC rent, you're paying for output. Same work, lower floor.
Do you sign strict NDAs and IP assignment?+
Yes. All engagements start with a mutual NDA + IP assignment. All code, specs, and design assets belong to your company from the moment they're created.
What stacks do you work in?+
Next.js, React, Vue, TypeScript, Node, Python, Go, Ruby. Databases: Postgres, Supabase, Mongo. Cloud: Vercel, AWS, GCP. AI: Claude, OpenAI, custom agents. We pick up your stack, not impose ours.
Can we hire your people away from you?+
We have a conversion-to-FTE path documented in our MSA. It's expensive but clean — and we've done it. We'd rather keep the engagement going, but we won't hold your team hostage.
How do you handle on-call and incidents?+
For pod-level engagements we join your on-call rotation for surfaces we built. For project-level work we ship and hand off. Spelled out in the MSA.
