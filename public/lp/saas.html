· SaaS questions

Everything your CTO wants to ask before the call.

Do you work on our existing codebase?

Yes — 80% of our SaaS work is inside existing codebases. We read the repo first, follow your conventions, respect your code review process.

How does the retainer actually work?

Monthly blocks of hours ($4.9k / $8.9k / $14.9k tiers). Unused hours roll over one month. Scale up or down month-to-month. No long contracts.

Will you sign our NDA & IP assignment?

Yes. We sign your NDA, your MSA, and assign all IP by default. We've been through legal review with several VC-backed teams.

How do you integrate with our internal team?

Shared Slack, shared GitHub, same standups. We're credited (internally) as contractors but present to clients as part of your team when needed.

What if we grow past needing you?

Ideal outcome. We help you write the job specs, pair with the first senior hire during onboarding, then ramp down. 60% of our retainers become "break glass in emergency" partnerships.
