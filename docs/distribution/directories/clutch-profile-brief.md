### Company description (usar este texto exacto — 1,850 caracteres)

```
LIVV Creative Studio is a boutique creative engineering studio based in Buenos Aires, Argentina. We build custom software, AI integrations, and digital products for founders and agencies across the United States, United Kingdom, and Europe.

Our team is senior-only. No juniors on client work. The same people who design ship the code, which means the product you get in production matches what was designed — with no handoff loss between teams.

Primary services:

Custom Software Development — business applications, internal tools, and SaaS MVPs built to fit your actual workflow rather than forcing your process into a generic SaaS tool. We have built and shipped Payper (a custom POS for hospitality), LegalFlow (legal case management), and PRTool (a creator partnership platform). These are in production with paying users — not mockups.

AI Integration — custom AI agents, RAG knowledge systems, and Anthropic Claude or OpenAI integrations added to existing products or built as standalone workflows. We have shipped PM Agent, an AI project management agent used internally and licensed to other studios.

Creative Engineering — Webflow, Framer, and Next.js marketing sites and product surfaces where design and development are the same team. We have shipped work for brands across the US, UK, and Latin America.

White-Label Partnerships — we operate as an invisible partner for agencies in the US and UK, shipping senior-quality work under the agency's brand under NDA.

Pricing is fixed-fee or retainer. Full cost transparency before we start. We are a boutique studio, not the cheapest option — but we are consistently faster, tighter on scope, and more honest about timelines than larger agencies at comparable quality.
```
